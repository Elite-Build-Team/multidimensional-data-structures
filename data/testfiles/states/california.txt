California is a state in the Pacific Region of the United States. With 39.6 million residents, California is the most populous U.S. 
state and the third-largest by area. The state capital is Sacramento. The Greater Los Angeles Area and the San Francisco Bay Area are the nation's second- and fifth-most 
populous 
urban regions, with 18.7 million and 9.7 million residents respectively. Los Angeles is California's most populous city, and the country's second-most populous, after New York City.
